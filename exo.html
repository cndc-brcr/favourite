MANILA, Philippines – Have you seen a boyband come out with 23 teasers, 2 concerts, and 2 music videos even before their actual debut?
Well, that’s how extra EXO is!

The 9-member Korean-Chinese boyband’s popularity in the Philippines peaked after their electronic dance music single “Ko Ko Bop” went viral as a challenge in 2017.

Six years after their 2012 debut, their fans called EXO-Ls now count to nearly 4.4 million in the official fan registry.

Called the “biggest Korean boyband in the world,” according to Dazed in 2016, EXO is currently a 9-member team with Suho, Kai, Sehun, Xiumin, Lay, Chen, D.O., Baekhyun, and Chanyeol.

‘Extra-terrestrial’ debut

EXO started as a 12-member group with two independent subgroups, EXO-K (which performs in Korean) and EXO-M (which performs in Mandarin). After several changes, their management SM Entertainment finalized the group’s name as EXO, inspired by the term “exoplanet".The boys attracted a lot of social media attention worldwide due to its unusual promotional campaigns. Before their formal debut, SM Entertainment released 23 teasers, 2 solo shows, and 2 music videos for EXO.The company even tapped mobile billboards around Seoul to promote the group.
